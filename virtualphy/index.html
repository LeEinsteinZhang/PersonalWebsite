About Us

VirtualPHY is a software development team in Ontario, Canada. Dedicated to developing physics-related simulation programs for mixed/augmented reality. The target group is physics enthusiasts and students as well as people more interested in exploration. Providing them with a fun physics learning experience with a more realistic simulation of the real physics world in a virtual world.
