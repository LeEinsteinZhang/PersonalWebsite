AR/MR designer

Waterloo, ON | Full-time

Job Responsibilities:
  • Responsible for the research and application of physical modeling and AR/MR technology, developing and optimizing model algorithms.
  • Based on project requirements, complete the creative design and implementation of AR/MR content.
  • Work with the team to follow up the entire product process from concept to market to ensure that technical implementation is consistent with project goals.
  • Promote technological innovation and continuously improve design quality and efficiency.
Job Requirements:
  • Strong professional background, bachelor degree or above, proficient in 3D modeling and AR/MR technology.
  • Active creative thinking, good project management skills and teamwork spirit.
  • Have in-depth understanding of technology and be able to independently solve technical problems.
  • Applicants with work experience in related fields will be given priority.
